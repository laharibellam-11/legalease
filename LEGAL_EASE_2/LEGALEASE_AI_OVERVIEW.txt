========================================================================
                      LEGALEASE-AI: PROJECT OVERVIEW
========================================================================

1. PROJECT MISSION
------------------
LegalEase-AI is a high-fidelity legal document intelligence platform 
designed to transform complex legal agreements into clear, actionable 
insights. It specializes in "landmine detection"—identifying high-risk 
clauses that traditional systems might overlook.

2. SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE & TECH STACK
------------------------------------
- Frontend: 
    * Framework: React with Vite (Fast & Modern)
    * Styling: Vanilla CSS & TailwindCSS (Apple-inspired aesthetic)
    * State Management: React Context (Auth, Document, Chat)
    * Icons: Lucide-React / React-Icons (Hi2)
    * Build Tool: Vite

- Backend:
    * Framework: FastAPI (Python 3.10+)
    * Database: MongoDB with Beanie ODM (Async Object-Document Mapper)
    * Authentication: JWT-based secure sessions
    * Task Management: Background Task integration

- AI & Machine Learning:
    * Orchestrator: Google Gemini 2.0 (Flash & Pro) for context and chat
    * Semantic Engine: LEGAL-BERT (nlpaueb/legal-bert-base-uncased)
    * Embeddings: HuggingFace (sentence-transformers)
    * Vector Store: Qdrant / Local Vector Implementation

3. CORE FEATURES
----------------
- Dual-Engine Risk Analysis (v8):
    * Rule-Based Engine (60% weight): Scans for mandatory missing clauses 
      and executes 15+ regex-based legal stress tests.
    * Semantic AI Engine (40% weight): Uses Legal-BERT to analyze clause 
      sentiment and deviation from industry standards.
    * Unified Scoring: (0.6 * Rule) + (0.4 * Semantic).

- Single Source of Truth (SSOT):
    * Risk data is calculated once and cached in the LegalDocument model.
    * The Dashboard, Analysis UI, and Chatbot all consume the same 
      schema-perfect risk object.

- Hybrid Clause Extraction:
    * Combines regex pattern matching for section headers with 
      Legal-BERT's semantic understanding to extract precisely defined 
      legal provisions.

- Context-Aware Chat Bot:
    * RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline.
    * Bypasses standard generation for "RISK" intents to render 
      interactive RiskAlerts directly inside the chat.

- Comprehensive Reporting:
    * Automated PDF/TXT report generation with risk summaries, 
      extracted clauses, and negotiation suggestions.

/ADMIN

    documents.

5. RISK SCORING MECHANISM
-------------------------
A) Presence Checks: HIGH (3 pts) for missing Confidentiality, Liability, 
   or Governing Law.
B) Logic Checks: MEDIUM/HIGH for unfavorable Termination, Indemnity, 
   or Auto-Renewal triggers.
C) Semantic Check: BERT score based on "unfairness" detection.

Mapping:
🟢 Low Risk (<25%)
🟡 Medium Risk (25-49%)
🔴 High Risk (>=50%)
